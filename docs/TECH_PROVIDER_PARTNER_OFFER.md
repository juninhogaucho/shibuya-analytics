# Tech Provider Partner Offer

Updated: 2026-03-29
Scope: canonical B2B offer for technology providers, prop-tech infrastructure vendors, brokers, and platform operators

## What We Sell

Shibuya is not just a direct-to-trader product.

For technology providers, Shibuya is a sell-through intelligence layer:
- trader-facing performance intelligence
- operator-facing risk and behavior intelligence
- optional expansion into PropOS for greenfield or replacement cases

This partner motion does not replace the core company motion.

We still sell directly to:
- props
- brokers
- firms that want Shibuya without an intermediary
- firms that want the full PropOS stack

Technology-provider partnerships are an additional channel, not a strategic retreat from direct B2B.

The first ask is not "replace your stack."

The first ask is:

"Let your customers buy Shibuya through you, and let Shibuya make your infrastructure more valuable."

## Who This Is For

Ideal partner profiles:
- prop-tech infrastructure providers
- brokers with account, portal, or CRM layers
- white-label technology vendors serving multiple firms
- trading platforms that want a differentiated intelligence layer

## What Their Customers Get

### Traders

- clearer feedback on discipline leaks and edge concentration
- session-by-session prescriptions instead of generic summaries
- a portable performance layer that can outlive one firm

### Props And Brokers

- a trader-facing product they can resell or bundle
- operator-side risk and behavior intelligence
- a stronger acquisition and retention story

## Offer Ladder

### 1. Supported By Shibuya

What the provider does:
- gives us a safe ingestion path, export format, or platform connector
- allows compatibility positioning

What the provider gets:
- "Shibuya-supported" compatibility badge
- mutual listing and co-marketing
- a low-friction way to test demand from their installed base

Use when:
- the partner wants minimal commitment
- the goal is proving adoption before deeper integration

### 2. Embedded Trader Intelligence

What we provide:
- Shibuya trader access sold through the partner
- co-branded or embedded activation path
- live trader workspace access for the partner's end customers

What the provider gets:
- new add-on revenue
- more trader retention and usage
- a differentiated story without rebuilding their core stack

Use when:
- the partner wants a sellable trader feature, not just a badge

### 3. Operator Intelligence

What we provide:
- cohort and account-level risk/behavior intelligence
- abuse and intervention signals
- operator-facing surfaces or APIs

What the provider gets:
- better internal risk tooling
- higher-value enterprise conversations with firms on their stack
- a second commercial wedge beyond trader-facing analytics

Use when:
- the partner wants firm-side value as well as trader-side value

### 4. PropOS Expansion

What we provide:
- full white-label operating system plus Shibuya intelligence

What the provider gets:
- a replacement or greenfield path when a customer wants the full stack

Use when:
- replacement is rational
- the partner or end customer wants a deeper operating system relationship

## Relationship To Direct Sales

The rule is:
- direct props and brokers can still buy Shibuya and PropOS from us
- technology providers can resell or embed Shibuya into their own installed base
- if a firm wants the full operating system, PropOS remains our direct full-stack offer

Partner distribution expands reach.

It does not replace direct selling.

## Recommended Commercial Structure

This is the opening structure, not a public rate card.

### Pilot

- non-exclusive
- 30 days
- zero integration fee if the partner gives:
  - one usable data path
  - and at least two customer introductions or one real pilot cohort
- otherwise EUR 3,000 setup for integration and launch support

### Embedded Trader Intelligence

Default commercial options:
- revenue share: 70 percent Shibuya / 30 percent partner on net collected Shibuya revenue through the partner channel
- wholesale: EUR 7 per active trader per month with a EUR 1,500 monthly floor

### Operator Intelligence

Default commercial option:
- EUR 2,500 monthly floor
- plus EUR 0.50 per monitored account

### PropOS Expansion

- custom pricing
- separate commercial process

## Positioning Rule

Do not pitch this as:
- "AI analytics"
- "just another dashboard"
- "replace your stack now"

Pitch it as:
- a distribution-friendly intelligence layer
- a way for the provider to monetize its installed base more deeply
- a way for firms on that stack to offer more value without migrating first

## The Core Partner Sentence

"Your firms keep your infrastructure. Shibuya gives them trader performance intelligence and operator risk intelligence that you can resell, embed, or bundle."

## Success Criteria

A partner deal is good when it creates:
- one real data path
- one real pilot cohort or one real design-partner firm
- one real commercial structure that can scale across the installed base

A partner deal is weak when it creates:
- vague collaboration language
- no data access
- no distribution commitment
- no commercial owner on their side
