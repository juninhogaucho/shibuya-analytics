# Tech Provider Outbound Playbook

Updated: 2026-03-29
Scope: B2B outreach and deal structure for Shibuya platform partnerships

## Objective

Close one non-exclusive technology-provider partnership that gives Shibuya:
- a real data path
- access to an installed base
- one commercial motion that does not depend on cold direct acquisition alone

This is an added channel.

We still sell:
- direct to props
- direct to brokers
- direct PropOS deployments

## Priority Targets

Target the provider layer first:
- prop-tech infrastructure vendors
- broker technology platforms
- white-label providers with multiple firms on one stack

The criteria are:
- installed base already exists
- integration path is technically feasible
- partner can distribute into multiple firms without one-off sales each time

## What We Want

Best-case outcome:
- provider agrees to a 30-day pilot
- provider gives one usable export or API path
- provider introduces two firms or one real cohort
- Shibuya launches as an embedded or co-sold intelligence layer

## What We Offer

Opening ladder:
1. compatibility and co-marketing
2. embedded trader intelligence
3. operator intelligence
4. PropOS expansion where it makes sense

## Recommended Outreach Angle

Do not lead with "we built a better prop stack."

Lead with:
- your customers can offer Shibuya without changing their stack
- you can resell or embed trader performance intelligence
- you can later unlock operator-side risk and behavior tooling from the same engine
- direct props and brokers can still buy from us; this simply adds another route into the market

## Recommended First Message

Subject:
Add Shibuya intelligence to your installed base

Body:

We are not approaching you to replace your stack on day one.

We built Shibuya as a trader-performance and operator-intelligence layer that can sit on top of existing prop or broker infrastructure. That lets your customers add differentiated trader feedback loops and firm-side intelligence without replatforming first.

The simplest version is a non-exclusive pilot:
- one usable data path
- two customer introductions or one pilot cohort
- Shibuya delivered as an embedded or co-sold intelligence layer

If it works, you gain a new sell-through product for your installed base. If it does not, nobody had to rip out their infrastructure to find out.

If that is worth a 20-minute conversation, we can show the exact partner ladder and pilot structure.

## Discovery Questions

Ask:
- how many firms are on your stack today
- whether they already expose exports, APIs, or webhook paths
- whether they prefer reseller, revenue-share, or platform-license models
- whether the first commercial wedge should be trader-facing or operator-facing
- who owns partner economics internally

## Non-Negotiables

Do not proceed if:
- they want exclusivity immediately
- they refuse usable data access
- they want free custom development without distribution or pilot commitment
- there is no commercial owner on the other side

## Pilot Exit Criteria

The pilot graduates only if it produces:
- working ingestion or connector path
- one live pilot cohort or design-partner firm
- measurable engagement or commercial intent
- an agreed post-pilot commercial structure
